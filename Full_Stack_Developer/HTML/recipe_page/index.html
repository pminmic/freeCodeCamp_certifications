Torrijas

Torrija, also known as French toast, is a dish made from a slice of bread soaked in milk, syrup, or wine, then dipped in egg and fried in a pan with oil. It is sweetened with honey, molasses, or sugar and flavored with cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • Bread (preferably from the previous day or special for torrijas)
  • Milk (about 500 ml)
  • Sugar (3-4 tablespoons)
  • Cinnamon stick (1 stick)
  • Lemon or orange peel (optional, to flavor the milk)
  • Eggs (2-3, depending on the amount of bread)
  • Mild olive oil or sunflower oil (for frying)
  • Sugar and ground cinnamon (for sprinkling)
  • Honey or syrup (optional, for extra sweetness)

Instructions

  1. Heat the milk – In a pot, warm the milk with sugar, cinnamon, and lemon/orange peel. Let it cool.
  2. Soak the bread – Cut the bread into thick slices and soak them in the milk for a few seconds.
  3. Dip in egg – Beat the eggs in a bowl and dip each slice of soaked bread.
  4. Fry – Heat oil in a pan and fry the slices until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Drain and sprinkle – Place them on paper towels, then sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Add honey – Drizzle with honey or syrup if you like.
