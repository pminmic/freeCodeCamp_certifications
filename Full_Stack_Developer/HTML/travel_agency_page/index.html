Trip to Scotland

Scotland offers a mix of history, nature, and culture. In Edinburgh, you can explore the iconic castle and walk down the Royal Mile. Glasgow is perfect for museums, live music, and vibrant nightlife. If you love nature, the Scottish Highlands offer breathtaking landscapes, with the chance to visit Loch Ness. The Isle of Skye is known for its scenic beauty, including the Fairy Pools. Whisky lovers can follow a distillery trail to taste some of the world’s best Scotch. History enthusiasts can visit castles like Eilean Donan, Stirling, and Dunnottar. For Harry Potter fans, the Jacobite Steam Train provides a magical journey. Finally, if you’re into golf, don’t miss St Andrews, the home of the sport.
